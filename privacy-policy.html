Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how JobTread Department Sync ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application to sync department data between JobTread and QuickBooks Online.

2. Information We Access

The Application accesses the following information from your connected services:

  • JobTread: Job information, custom field data (specifically the Department field), and job identifiers
  • QuickBooks Online: Transaction data (invoices, bills, expenses), class/category information, and transaction identifiers

3. How We Use Information

We use the accessed information solely to:

  • Match JobTread jobs to QuickBooks Online transactions
  • Update QuickBooks Online transaction classes based on JobTread department values
  • Maintain accurate synchronization between the two systems

4. Data Storage and Security

The Application:

  • Does not store any of your business data on external servers
  • Processes data locally on your computer
  • Uses secure API connections (HTTPS) for all communications
  • Stores only necessary authentication credentials locally on your machine

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your information
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Access your data except when you explicitly run the synchronization

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect the Application from JobTread and QuickBooks Online at any time
  • Delete any locally stored credentials
  • Request information about what data the Application has accessed

7. Third-Party Services

This Application integrates with:

  • JobTread: Subject to JobTread's Privacy Policy
  • QuickBooks Online: Subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services.
